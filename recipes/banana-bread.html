Banana Bread

Description

Use this recipe to get rid of those pesky overripe bananas. A few simple steps will satisfy everyone. Perfect warmed up with butter.

Ingredients

  • 1 c. sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 c. dates, chopped (optional)
  • 1 1/2 c. flour
  • 1/4 c. butter, softened
  • 3 bananas, mashed
  • 1 tsp. baking soda

Steps

  1. Cream butter and sugar.
  2. Add beaten eggs.
  3. Add remaining ingredients.
  4. Bake in a loaf pan for one hour at 350 degrees F.
