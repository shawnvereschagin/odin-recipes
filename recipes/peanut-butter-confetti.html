Peanut Butter Confetti

Description

This is a perfect recipe for the peanut butter lover in your family! Ooey-gooey and oh so yummy. These are also gluten free. A perfect treat for all.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 c. butter
  • 1/2 c. peanut butter
  • 1 c. butterscotch chips
  • 5 c. mini marshmallows

Steps

  1. Melt butter and peanut butter over low heat.
  2. Stir in chips until melted.
  3. Cool until you can hold your hand on the pot.
  4. Add marshmallows and stir until coated.
  5. Pack down in a 9x9 pan that has been lined with wax paper. Refrigerate.
